Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

To place an order, browse our menu, select the items you want, and follow the checkout process. You can order online or visit our restaurant.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cash for in-person orders. For online orders, credit card and PayPal are preferred.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within a certain radius of our restaurant. Check our delivery areas on the menu page or contact us for more details.
